Expert Care for Women's Health

Our Dr. Saranya MBBS, MD (Gynaecology) is one of the leading gynecologists in Kallakurichi. We prioritize PATIENT SAFETY and provide the BEST TREATMENT with home-like hospitality. Experience professional care at reasonable costs.

Women Care

Gynecological diseases, vagina infection, hysterectomy, pregnancy issues, cervix and uterus, Dysmenorrhea, Amenorrhoea.

Infertility Treatment

Male and female infertility diagnosis, consultation and treatment. Assisted from diagnosis to until child birth.

Pregnancy Care

Prenatal, during pregnancy and Postpartum Care. Tests, consultation, mother health monitoring, and fetus growth and health monitoring.
